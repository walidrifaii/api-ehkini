Zero Tolerance for Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

We have a strict ZERO-TOLERANCE policy against any form of child sexual abuse, exploitation, or grooming. This applies to all content, including Stories, Chat messages, profile pictures, and Virtual Gifts interactions.

Explicitly Prohibited Activities

  • ❌ Creating, uploading, sharing, or requesting any content that depicts or describes child sexual abuse (Child Sexual Abuse Material – CSAM).
  • ❌ Grooming, sexually exploiting, or attempting to contact a minor for sexual purposes – regardless of whether the conversation starts inside or outside the app.
  • ❌ Sharing links, usernames, or references to external platforms that host CSAM or facilitate child exploitation.
  • ❌ Using the app’s chat or gift features to trade, sell, or promote any form of child exploitation.
  • ❌ Any AI‑generated or synthetic content that depicts minors in sexually suggestive or abusive scenarios.

Enforcement & Reporting

When we become aware of any CSAE violation, we will:

  • ✅ Immediately remove the offending content (Story, message, gift, or profile).
  • ✅ Permanently ban the user account without warning.
  • ✅ Report confirmed CSAM to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) and, where required, to local law enforcement.
  • ✅ Preserve all relevant evidence for legal authorities.

Child Safety Point of Contact

To report any concern related to child safety (including suspected grooming, CSAM, or inappropriate contact with minors), please contact our dedicated Child Safety Officer immediately:

📧 Child Safety Email: childsafety@storychatgifts.com
⏱️ We will acknowledge your report within 24 hours and take action within 48 hours.

In‑App Reporting Mechanism

Our app provides a “Report” button on every Story, Chat message, and user profile. Users can tap the three dots (⋮) or flag icon to report any suspicious or abusive content. All child safety reports are escalated to our Child Safety Officer. You do not need to log in to use the in‑app reporting feature – it is available to all users.

⚠️ Legal obligation: We comply with the Google Play Child Safety Standards policy, the US PROTECT Act, and all applicable local laws. Any user who attempts to bypass our moderation or share CSAM will be permanently banned and reported to the authorities.

🔒 Privacy Policy

2. Information We Collect

To provide Stories, Chat, and Gifts, we collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account & Profile: name, username, email address, profile picture, and optional bio.
  • Stories Content: photos, videos, captions, location tags (if granted), and metadata such as timestamps and view counts. Stories are automatically deleted after 24h, but are stored on our servers during that period.
  • Chat Messages: text, images, voice notes, and files shared in direct messages or groups. Message history is retained until you delete it or your account is removed.
  • Gift Transactions: records of virtual gifts sent/received, purchase history, and transaction IDs. No financial or credit card data is processed by us – all payments go through Apple App Store / Google Play Store.
  • Device & Usage Data: device type, OS version (Android/iOS), unique identifiers (IDFV/GAID with consent), app version, crash logs, and feature interaction analytics.
  • Optional Permissions: camera, photo library, microphone, and notifications (used for creating stories and chat features). Permission can be revoked in device settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to operate, improve, and secure the App:

  • ✓ Deliver Stories, Chat, and Gifts functionality (e.g., showing your story to followers, delivering messages, processing gift animations).
  • ✓ Personalize content and suggest friends based on activity (with opt-out available).
  • ✓ Moderate prohibited content using automated + human review to ensure community safety.
  • ✓ Analyze usage trends, fix bugs, and enhance performance on both Android and iOS.
  • ✓ Comply with legal obligations, enforce our Terms, and prevent fraud/abuse.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: cloud hosting (e.g., AWS, Firebase), analytics (privacy-preserving), push notification providers. All processors sign data protection agreements.
  • Legal & safety: if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.
  • With your consent: e.g., when you choose to cross-post a story to other platforms.
  • Business transfers: in case of merger or acquisition, you will be notified.

Stories & Chat visibility: Your stories are visible to your chosen audience (followers, friends, etc.) and chat messages are only visible to conversation participants – except if reported for moderation.

5. Your Privacy Rights (GDPR / CCPA / Global)

Depending on your region, you have the following rights, which we readily honor:

  • Right to access & portability: obtain a copy of your data.
  • Right to rectification: correct inaccurate profile information.
  • Right to deletion ("right to be forgotten"): delete your account and associated data (stories, chat logs, gift history).
  • Right to restrict/object processing: opt out of non-essential analytics.
  • CCPA specific: Right to know what personal data we collect, request deletion, and opt-out of any “sale” (we do not sell data).
  • Parental/guardian rights: for children under applicable age of consent, parents may request deletion of child's data.

To exercise any right, contact privacy@storychatgifts.com or use the in-app "Data Request" feature.

6. Children’s Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K)

Our app is not directed at children under 13 (or higher age under local law, e.g., 16 in certain EU countries). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us and we will delete it immediately. Users between 13 and 18 must have parental permission where required by law.

7. Data Retention & Security

We retain your information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Stories are deleted automatically after 24 hours. Chat messages remain until you delete them or close your account. Gift transaction records are kept for tax/legal compliance (up to 7 years). We implement industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3), data access controls, and regular security audits for both Android and iOS environments.
